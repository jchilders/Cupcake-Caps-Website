About Us

Cupcake Caps were dreamed up by Brooke Brooks, the single mom of elementary age twins.

The story is that she needed to send party cupcakes with the kiddos that would survive the day (the cupcakes, that is) and arrive at the late afternoon party still looking fabulous.

Knowing that buttercream decorated cupcakes would never survive, she pondered on what would be tasty, not-fragile and gorgeous.  Drawing on years of cooking experience, she came up with Cupcake Caps.

As with so many other things, Cupcake Caps were created out of necessity and love. : )
